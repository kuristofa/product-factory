# Grand Slam Offer — Output

Here's the low-ticket entry offer based on our session. Built as an easy first "yes."

## The Offer: "The First 1,000 Subscribers"

A step-by-step mini-kit that helps small creators grow their first 1,000 email subscribers from
the audience they already have — without ads, funnels, or a big following.

## Who this is for

Coaches, creators, and small-business owners who post on Instagram but have a tiny or non-existent
email list. They know "the money is in the list" but feel behind, overwhelmed by tech, and unsure
what to even offer people to get them to subscribe. They're time-poor and allergic to anything that
smells like complicated marketing software.

## The core problem

They're renting their audience on social and own nothing. One algorithm change and they're invisible.
But every "grow your list" guide assumes ads, landing-page builders, or a lead magnet they don't have
time to make — so they stall and never start.

## The promise (dream outcome)

In two weeks, using only the audience and tools they already have, they can set up a simple subscribe
system and run one "list sprint" that gets them their first few hundred subscribers — on the way to
1,000. The win we're selling: seeing real people join their list from a single Reel.

## What's inside (the value stack)

The core is a short, do-it-with-me kit:

- **The 3-Line Opt-In** — a fill-in-the-blank formula for a subscribe offer people actually want,
  built in 15 minutes without a designer or a lead-magnet marathon. This is the centerpiece.
- **The List Sprint Plan** — a 7-day content + DM plan that turns existing followers into subscribers.
- **The No-Tech Setup Guide** — the simplest free way to collect emails and deliver the opt-in,
  with zero funnel software.

Designed to be done on one platform, one step at a time, over a weekend plus a sprint week.

## Bonuses

- **20 Opt-In Hooks** — swipeable one-liners that make people want to subscribe. (Kills the
  "I don't know what to say" objection.)
- **The Welcome Email Template** — the one email that turns a new subscriber into a fan, ready to paste.

Both are small accelerators — neither is bigger than the core kit.

## Order bump

At checkout, offer a **$11 "Reels-to-Subscribers Pack"** — 10 done-for-you Reel scripts whose only
job is to drive email signups. A natural, low-cost add-on to the core system.

## Where this can go later (upsells — don't build yet)

Once validated: a "List to Launch" program that monetizes the list, and a monthly membership with
fresh monthly sprints. Hold these until the front-end proves out.

## Objections we need to handle

- "I don't have a lead magnet / time to make one." (The 3-Line Opt-In needs no lead magnet.)
- "I'm not techy." (No-Tech Setup Guide uses one free tool, no funnels.)
- "My audience is too small." (The sprint works with even a few hundred followers.)
- "Email feels old / dead." (It's the one channel they actually own.)

## Price & why it's a no-brainer

Price it at **$24**. A single client or product sale down the line pays it back many times, and a list
is an asset they keep forever. Cheaper than one month of most funnel tools.

## Guarantee / how we stay honest

No subscriber-count guarantees and no income promises — results depend on their audience and effort.
We promise the system and that they'll have a working subscribe setup by the end. No fake scarcity.

## Voice / tone

Encouraging, plain-spoken, a little bit "you've got this." Talk like a friend who's a step ahead,
not a marketing bro. No hype.

## DM automation

We run the keyword-to-DM signups through **ManyChat**.

## Delivery

Instant digital delivery: a Google Drive folder with PDFs, the fill-in worksheets, and a short
welcome video. Access emailed automatically right after purchase.
