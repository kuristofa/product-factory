# Grand Slam Offer — Output

Here's the offer I've put together based on our conversation. It's built to be a no-brainer
low-ticket entry product.

## The Offer: "The Etsy Listing Fix"

A plug-and-play mini-kit that turns dead Etsy listings into ones that actually get found and
bought — without the seller having to learn SEO or hire anyone.

## Who this is for

This is aimed at handmade and craft Etsy sellers who've made a handful of sales but have
mostly-silent shops. They're usually solo, creative rather than technical, easily overwhelmed by
"marketing," and short on time because they're the one making the product. They don't want to
become marketers — they just want their existing listings to work.

## The core problem

Their listings are invisible. They pour hours into their products and photos, but their titles,
tags, and descriptions aren't written the way Etsy's search actually reads them — so buyers never
see them. They blame the algorithm, drop their prices, or quietly give up.

## The promise (dream outcome)

Within a weekend, they can rewrite their top listings so they start showing up in Etsy search and
converting browsers into buyers — using a repeatable formula, not guesswork. The goal we're
selling: their first "where did this sale come from?!" moment from a listing they fixed.

## What's inside (the value stack)

The core is a short, do-it-with-me kit:

- **The Listing Formula** — a fill-in-the-blank framework for titles, tags, and descriptions that
  matches how Etsy search ranks listings. This is the heart of it.
- **The 15-Minute Listing Audit** — a simple checklist they run on any existing listing to find
  exactly what's killing its visibility.
- **The Keyword Grab worksheet** — a dead-simple way to find the search terms real buyers type,
  without paid tools.

Everything's designed to be finished over a weekend on one or two of their best products first.

## Bonuses

- **50 High-Converting Title Templates** — swipeable title structures for common product types,
  so they never stare at a blank field. (Removes the "I don't know what to write" objection.)
- **The Photo Order Cheat Sheet** — the ideal order to arrange listing photos so the first image
  earns the click.

Neither bonus is bigger than the core kit — they just make it faster to get a result.

## Order bump

At checkout, offer a **$9 "Tag Vault"** — a categorized bank of 300+ proven Etsy tags they can
copy straight into listings. Small, obvious add-on that complements the formula.

## Where this can go later (upsells — don't build yet)

Once this validates, natural next steps are a "Full Shop Makeover" done-with-you program, and a
monthly membership with seasonal keyword drops. Hold these until the front-end sells.

## Objections we need to handle

- "I'm not techy / I don't get SEO." (The whole thing is fill-in-the-blank, no jargon.)
- "I don't have time." (Designed for one weekend, one listing at a time.)
- "I've tried tips before and nothing changed." (This is a repeatable formula, not random tips.)
- "Will this work for MY niche?" (The formula is niche-agnostic; templates cover common types.)

## Price & why it's a no-brainer

Price it at **$29**. One extra sale a week from a single fixed listing pays for it many times
over, and most sellers have dozens of listings to apply it to. The math is obvious to them.

## Guarantee / how we stay honest

No income promises — we don't guarantee sales, because results depend on their products and
effort. We can promise the framework and that they'll have rewritten, search-ready listings by the
end. Keep all claims grounded; no fake scarcity.

## Voice / tone

Warm, encouraging, plain-English. Talk to them like a fellow maker who figured this out, not a
marketing guru. No hype.

## Delivery

Instant digital delivery: a Google Drive folder with PDFs and the fill-in worksheets, plus a short
welcome video. Access emailed automatically right after purchase.
